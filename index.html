Billenial

The very first product I ever worked on was on my own app while in my last year of College in 2017. I saw a need in the market, especially with college students. Millenials are not loyal to one bank like their Gen Z parents, they also had many bills because of the countless subscription options they had like Netflix, Spotify, etc. College students needed a place to organize and keep track of their banks and their bills. Lastly, it was a lot of work to find scholarship and grant opportunities, I wanted Billenial to be the place where students could find them as easy as scrolling through like they do on Instagram. Discover our journey, from conception to winning several pitch competitions.
